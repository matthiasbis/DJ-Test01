Meet the team

Our Leadership
Tatiana Freire, Founder and CEO, is our customer and business champion. She works closely with product managers and our global teams to transform data into the perfect customer experience for you. Her passion is to empower pharma teams with precise market insights & analytics and boost your product innovation and targeted messaging.

Matthias Petri, Executive Chairman, brings 25 years experience in Healthcare and Business Strategy to the team. With a focus on our global partnerships, AI technology and operational excellence, Matthias drives our vision and pushes the boundaries on what Data Jukebox can deliver to our clients.

​Our Team
​​Our global team works around the clock to deliver the best solutions for you. From product development and client brief to solution delivery our highly skilled and motivated team supports you in all steps along our joint journey.
